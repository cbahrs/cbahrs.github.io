What I do

U.S. Coast Guard | Sector Lake Michigan

I joined the Coast Guard in March of 2019 because I wanted a career where I could make a positive difference every day. I chose to become an operations specialist. As an operations specialist, we monitor and respond to all cases (primarily search and rescue) in the area where we are stationed. I was stationed at Sector Lake Michigan in Milwaukee in September of 2019.

Full-time Student | University of Wisconsin - Madison

The more I learned about my job the more I realized how much geographic information systems played a part in our effectiveness during active cases. This led me to pursue a degree in geographic information systems and learn as much as I can so that I will be able to apply it to my career in the U.S. Coast Guard and eventually a career in GIS.
